About Me

My Story

I’m a UI designer with a diverse background. On one hand, my degree in psychology and experience in sales strategy has helped develop my keen interest in what drives and affects individuals’ thought processes. On the other hand, my background in makeup artistry and wedding planning has inspired me to tap into my eye for beauty and my passion for creativity. Together, these experiences have led me to the world of UI - a place where I can combine my fascination and knowledge of the human response with my talent for creativity and visual design.

I currently call Los Angeles, California my home, but I was born and raised in Tokyo, Japan. My favorite activities include thrilling hikes (especially trails in Hawaii) and spending hours watching cute dog videos while hoping to one day have a puppy of my own.
