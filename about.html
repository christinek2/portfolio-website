About Me

My Story

I’m a UI designer with a diverse background in psychology, sales strategy, cosmetology, and wedding planning. My journey in a variety of fields has led me to the world of UI. From coordinating and designing weddings with stressed out brides in Hawaii, performing my makeup magic on over a hundred women and men, working intimately with autistic children on behavioral issues, and finally to designing a food distribution company’s website, social media, and sales material, I discovered my passion lies in helping people transform their ideas into appealing and intuitive designs. Combining both my psychology background and eye for creativity, my goal is to help build and design user-centric digital products that positively impact and transform the lives of individuals.

I currently call Los Angeles, California my home, but I was born and raised in Tokyo, Japan. My favorite activities include thrilling hikes (especially trails in Hawaii) and I can easily spend hours watching cute dog videos and hoping to oneday have a puppy of my own.
